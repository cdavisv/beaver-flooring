## Keyword Clusters

- Core local terms: `plumber beaverton`, `plumbing services beaverton`, `portland metro plumber`
- Residential terms: `home plumbing repair`, `residential plumber near me`
- Commercial terms: `commercial plumbing contractor portland`, `property management plumber`
- Emergency terms: `emergency plumber beaverton`, `24 hour plumbing help`
- Drain terms: `drain cleaning beaverton`, `drain repair portland metro`
- Water heater terms: `water heater repair beaverton`, `water heater replacement portland metro`

## Internal Linking Rules

- Home links to every top-level page plus top four money pages
- Services hub links to all service detail pages and guides
- Each service detail page links to FAQ, contact, and one related guide
- Guides link back to relevant service pages and contact
- Testimonials hub links to related service pages

## Structured Data Plan

- Global: `Organization`, `LocalBusiness`, `Plumber`, `WebSite`
- Home: `LocalBusiness`, `FAQPage`
- Services hub: `ItemList`
- Service pages: `Service`
- Testimonials page: `Review`, `AggregateRating`
- FAQ: `FAQPage`
- Contact: `ContactPage`, `LocalBusiness`

## SEO Content Notes

- Use service-area references naturally in headings, intro copy, and CTA bands
- Keep H1 unique on every page
- Build guide content to answer high-intent questions clearly before pitching services
- Use testimonials with service labels so they can support both proof and internal linking

## Tracking Defaults

- Track phone click CTA
- Track emergency CTA
- Track form submissions
- Track map interaction if embedded

## Canonical And Indexing Defaults

- Self-canonicalize every indexable page
- Noindex legal pages only if legal counsel or business owner prefers; default is indexable
- Avoid duplicate service-area doorway pages until unique content volume exists
